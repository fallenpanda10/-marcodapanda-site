# 3‑Minute Matcha Pause

1. Sit comfortably and hold a warm cup in both hands.
2. Breathe in for 4, out for 6 while noticing the warmth.
3. Take one mindful sip and name one small thing you’re grateful for.

*Notes: Adapt to standing or walking if needed. No matcha required — any warm cup works.*
